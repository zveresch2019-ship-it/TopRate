‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How to add a player?
Go to the "Rating" tab and click the "Add Player" button. Enter the player's name and initial rating.
How to save match results?
On the "Matches" tab, select players for each team (by pressing buttons A and B), enter the score, and click "Save Match". Player ratings will be updated automatically.
How does the rating calculation work?
The rating is calculated based on the Elo system, taking into account the difference in team strength, match result, and number of goals. The winning team gains points, the losing team loses points.
Can I export data?
Yes, on the "Home" tab, click the "Export Data" button. You can share the list of players and their statistics.
How to start a new season?
On the home page, click the "New Season" button. All player ratings will be reset to 1500, but match history will be preserved.
Is data stored locally?
Yes, all data is stored only on your device. We do not collect or transmit your information to third parties.

üîí Privacy

The TopRate app does not collect, store, or transmit user personal data. All data (players, matches, statistics) is stored exclusively on your device.
